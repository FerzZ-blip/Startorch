# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors exploring the Startorch Academy worldbuilding site. Imagined as prospective scholars, researchers, and curious travelers discovering the Spacetrek Collective's campus. They want both atmospheric immersion (what does this place feel like?) and practical orientation (what's here, where is it, what happens in each facility?).

## Product Purpose

A static HTML/CSS worldbuilding site for the fictional Startorch Academy research institute. The campus page exists to give visitors a sense of place — a tour of the physical and conceptual grounds of the academy. It should feel like exploring a real research compound: facilities, landmarks, zones of activity.

## Brand Personality

Worldbuilding-first, grounded, arcane-scientific, warm-dark aesthetic. Voice is authoritative but inviting — like a terminal interface crossed with an academic prospectus.

Three words: **grounded, arcane, expansive**

## Anti-references

- Generic academic sites with hero photos, white backgrounds, and blue CTAs
- AI-slop patterns: glassmorphism, gradient text, side-stripe borders
- Stock photography or cookie-cutter SaaS card rows
- Light/parchment sections in interior pages (dark theme throughout)

## Design Principles

1. **Worldbuilding-first.** Every element reinforces the fiction. Terminal UI, warm dark tones, space for atmosphere.
2. **Explore, don't lecture.** Layout should invite scanning and discovery, not monotonous lists.
3. **Practical clarity beneath the atmosphere.** Atmospheric top section, clear information architecture below.
4. **One page, many rhythms.** Vary pacing: wide establishing shot, then dense grid, then terminal readout, then close.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast on all text
- Reduced motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`
- No essential information conveyed through color alone
