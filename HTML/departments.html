Spacetrek Collective

The Spacetrek Collective is a non-profit international scientific organization jointly established by various nations and organizations of Solaris-3. It oversees the research, development, and construction work in Lahai-Roi. They built the Research Institute, Startorch Academy, Starward Riseway in the vast space beneath the frostlands. Its core mission is the study of the Exostrider for the purposes of advancing and re-instigating space travel technology.
