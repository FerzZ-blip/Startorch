Exostrider

The Exostrider is a giant, Resonance-powered humanoid developed by the Startorch Academy. Designed as an exploration and combat support unit, its mechanical body is capable of operating in terrain inaccessible to ordinary humans.

Spesifications
  • Type: Giant Humanoid Unit
  • Power Source: Resonance Core
  • Height: 12m Class
Research Goals
  • Protection Unit For Humanity
  • Combat And Exploration Unit
  • First Giant Humanoid With Pilot System
Development Notes

The Exostrider represents Startorch Academy's greatest breakthrough in humanoid construction. Pilots must have high Resonance compatibility to fully operate it.

Expedition Frostlands

Expedition Frostlands is a Startorch Academy field exploration mission to an extremely frozen region. The goal is to collect rare Resonance data found only in sub-zero environments.

Mission Parameters
  • Location: Roya Frostlands
  • Duration: 17 Months
  • Team Size: Around 87 Researchers
Research Goals
  • Explore Unknown Map
  • Collect Resource For The Lab
  • Documenting Unknown Flora And Fauna
Field Notes

The expedition team faced extreme conditions with temperatures reaching -60°C. However, initial findings indicate a previously unrecorded concentration of Resonance Energy.

Spacetrek Collective

The Spacetrek Collective is a non-profit international scientific organization jointly established by various nations and organizations of Solaris-3. It oversees the research, development, and construction work in Lahai-Roi. They built the Research Institute, Startorch Academy, Starward Riseway in the vast space beneath the frostlands. Its core mission is the study of the Exostrider for the purposes of advancing and re-instigating space travel technology.
