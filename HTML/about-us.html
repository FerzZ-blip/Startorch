Overview

Founded by the Spacetrek Collective, the academy is a premier institution that specifically recruits Resonators, with an ambitious ultimate goal: to reach the stars. Unlike faction-affiliated institutions elsewhere, Startorch Academy's identity is rooted in pure research—its very name suggests the "fire of inquiry" as its guiding principle. Its official lore highlights the academy's foundation in the open flow of knowledge and cultural exchange, fostering innovation beyond traditional faction boundaries.

Spacetrek Collective

The Spacetrek Collective is a non-profit international scientific organization jointly established by various nations and organizations of Solaris-3. It oversees the research, development, and construction work in Lahai-Roi. They built the Research Institute, Startorch Academy, Starward Riseway in the vast space beneath the frostlands. Its core mission is the study of the Exostrider for the purposes of advancing and re-instigating space travel technology.
