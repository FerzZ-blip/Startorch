Core Principles

Typography-Driven

Academic serif body (like Garamond or Tiempos) with a sharp monospace for interface elements. Editorial hierarchy — pull quotes, footnotes, marginalia.

Warm Dark + Paper

Deep warm charcoal backgrounds (not cool gray) transitioning into cream/ivory paper tones. Amber and resonance blue as accent colors.

Technology as Detail

Interface elements don't dominate — they're embedded. Think terminal windows in the margins, data readouts within academic layouts. Function as ornament.

Anti-AI-Slop Measures

What We're Doing Instead

  • Serif typography with real hierarchy (not Inter/ sans-default)
  • No gradients, no glassmorphism, no heavy shadows
  • No generic stock photography — photoless or specific archival imagery
  • Data/interface elements serve the lore, not decoration
  • Warm tones avoid the generic cool-dark-tech look
  • Dense layouts — not sparse hero + 3 cards + footer

What We're Avoiding

  • Tailwind default SaaS template look
  • Fade-in-up scroll animations on everything
  • "Revolutionize / Empower / Supercharge" copy
  • Gradient hero sections
  • Generic tech illustrations (people in hoodies)
  • Cookie-cutter card grids
